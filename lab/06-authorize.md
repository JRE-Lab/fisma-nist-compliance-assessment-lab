+# Lab 06: Authorize (RMF Step 5)
+
+## Purpose
+
+Prepare the authorization package and support the Authorizing Official's risk decision.
+
+## Objectives
+
+- Compile key artifacts for the authorization package.
+- Summarize residual risk and POA&M items.
+- Draft a recommendation for the ATO decision.
+
+## Tasks
+
+1. Assemble the SSP, SAR summary, POA&M, and categorization memo.
+2. Summarize residual risk and proposed mitigations.
+3. Draft an authorization recommendation memo.
+
+## Deliverables
+
+- Authorization package checklist
+- ATO recommendation memo
+- Updated POA&M with timelines
+
+## Checkpoint questions
+
+- Which risks are accepted vs. mitigated?
+- What conditions or milestones should be included in the ATO?
