+# Lab 01: Prepare (RMF Step 0)
+
+## Purpose
+
+Define the organizational context, governance, and risk management strategy that will guide the assessment.
+
+## Objectives
+
+- Identify organizational risk tolerance and key stakeholders.
+- Establish the assessment scope and assumptions.
+- Document the system mission and business impact.
+
+## Tasks
+
+1. Describe the mission/business objectives the CCP system supports.
+2. Identify key dependencies (cloud provider, identity provider, logging platform).
+3. Draft assumptions and constraints that will influence control selection.
+4. Define a risk tolerance statement (low, moderate, high appetite).
+
+## Deliverables
+
+- Risk tolerance statement
+- System mission and business impact statement
+- Assumptions and constraints list
+
+## Tips
+
+- Use the mission statement later when justifying impact levels.
+- Keep assumptions measurable (e.g., “99.9% availability target”).
