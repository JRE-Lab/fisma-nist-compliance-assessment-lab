+# Lab 05: Assess (RMF Step 4)
+
+## Purpose
+
+Evaluate whether controls are implemented correctly, operating as intended, and producing the desired outcome.
+
+## Objectives
+
+- Develop assessment procedures.
+- Identify control weaknesses and document findings.
+- Update the POA&M and produce a SAR summary.
+
+## Tasks
+
+1. Select a subset of controls to assess (minimum of 8).
+2. Describe assessment methods (examine, interview, test).
+3. Record findings and recommendations.
+4. Update the POA&M with remediation actions.
+
+## Deliverables
+
+- Security Assessment Report (SAR) summary
+- Updated POA&M
+- List of assessed controls and methods
+
+## Tips
+
+- Tie findings to evidence and control requirements.
+- Prioritize findings based on impact and likelihood.
